The Dubliner Irish Pub and Cafe

The Dubliner Irish Pub and Cafe is a locally owned and operated restaurant located in Redmond.
We are open Monday-Saturday from 11AM-9PM.
We offer a variety of different menu items and taps to choose from.

The Dubliner Irish Pub and Cafe is family friendly, so feel free to bring along your family enjoy a hearty meal.

Enjoy a wide selection of draft beers, whiskeys, and cocktails.
